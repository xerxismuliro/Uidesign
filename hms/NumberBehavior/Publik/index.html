About Factorials

The factorial of a non-negative integer n is the product of all positive integers less than or equal to n.

Example: 5! = 5 × 4 × 3 × 2 × 1 = 120

About Chromatic Numbers

Chromatic analysis explores patterns in digital roots and special number relationships.

This helps reveal mathematical patterns that aren't immediately obvious.
